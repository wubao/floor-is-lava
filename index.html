📋 HOW TO PLAY

🎮 Desktop Controls

⬆️
Space/A — Jump. Down+Jump = short hop.
⬅️
Arrow Keys / Q & D — Move. Walk off either edge into blank void — use a parachute to survive the fall!
🧗
Climber mode (toggle on start screen): player sits lower on screen, 10 platforms always visible above. Frogger mode: player centred — you can't see above until you jump!
⏸
P — Pause. 1=Boost, 2=Skip, 4=Book.

📱 Mobile Touch

👆
Tap left=jump left, right=jump right, top=jump up, near player=short hop.
👉
Drag = move player until you lift finger.
🤏
Pinch in = zoom out (see more). Pinch out = zoom in. See PINCH_ZOOM in code to adjust.
🎯
Power Wheel bottom center: tap quadrant to use that power.

🌋 Lava & Difficulty

📊
The HUD shows 🌋 +X plts below — how many platforms the lava is below you. Turns red when lava is close!
🌋
Rises 1.8 platforms/sec at start. Every 25 levels speed increases by 25%, up to a hard cap. If you climb more than 15 platforms above lava, it surges up to within 5 platforms of you!
📐
Higher levels: platforms shift sideways, bigger gaps, narrower platforms.

🪙 On Death

🎲
Play Rock Paper Scissors (you win 9/10 chance!) to keep your coins. Lose = coins go to Piggy Bank.
🐷
Piggy Bank holds lost coins. Win mini-games to reclaim them.
