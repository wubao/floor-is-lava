📋 HOW TO PLAY

🎮 QUICK DEMO

Space=Jump | ⬇️+Space=Half hop | ➡️⬅️=Move | ⬆️=Use selected power | 1=Boost 2=Skip 3=Book 4=Ship 5=Parachute 6=Torch

🎮 Desktop Controls

⬆️
Space — Jump. ⬇️+Space = short hop. ⬆️ Arrow = use selected power from wheel.
⬅️
← → Arrow Keys — Move left/right.
🔢
1=Jump Boost · 2=Skip · 3=The Book · 4=Lava Ship · 5=Parachute · 6=Torch. ⬇️+1–6 = Buy that power.
⏸
P — Pause/unpause.

📱 Mobile Touch

👆
Tap left side=move left, right side=move right, top half=jump, near player=short hop.
👉
Drag = slide player sideways until you lift your finger.
🤏
Pinch in = zoom out (see more platforms). Pinch out = zoom in (bigger view).
🎯
Power Wheel — bottom-left corner. Tap a quadrant to use that power-up instantly.

🎮 Game Modes

🧗
Climber — You can see 10 platforms above. Great for beginners and high scores.
🐸
Frogger — Blind mode. You can only see 2 platforms above. Platforms still exist above — jump to find them!
⚔️
Versus — Race 3 AI bots (Roxy, Blaze, Zippy) to the highest platform. Death costs 30 coins.
🌋
Volcano Chronicles — Story mode. Rescue artifacts from real-world volcanoes. Each volcano's elevation = your platform target.

🌋 Lava & Platforms

📈
Lava rises faster every 25 levels. If you climb more than 25 platforms above lava, it surges up to within 5 of you!
🟥
Red/Blue/Yellow — normal. Green — has a 🪙 coin on it. Purple — kills you after a few seconds, jump off! ❄️ Ice — slippery!
🧊
Stalagmites hang from platforms near the lava — they fall and slow lava when they hit it.
🌙
Night mode activates at level 20. Use a Torch Battery to restore vision.
📐
At higher levels platforms shift sideways, gaps grow wider, and platforms get narrower.

🪙 Death & Coins

🎲
On death, play Rock Paper Scissors (90% win rate!) to keep your coins. Lose = coins go to Piggy Bank.
🐷
Piggy Bank — holds coins lost on death. Win Piggy mini-games (Memory, Math, RPS) to reclaim them.
📺
Lost RPS? Watch an ad to save your coins instead of sending them to the Piggy.

🛍️ Power-Ups (buy in shop or press ⬇️+key)

⬆️
Jump Boost 20🪙 — Super-high jump. Stack up to 20. Press 1 or wheel top-left.
💫
Platform Skip 20🪙 — Teleport to 5 platforms above. 5 uses. Press 2 or wheel top-right.
📖
The Book 100🪙 — Big head! Pulls the platform above to you. Press 3 or wheel bottom-left.
🚀
Lava Ship 150🪙 — Rocket 50 platforms upward instantly! Press 4 or wheel bottom-right.
🪂
Parachute 50🪙 — Auto-deploys when you fall off the edge. Slow safe descent. Press 5.
🔋
Torch Battery 50🪙 — Refills torch to 100%, fighting off night mode. Press 6.

✏️ Level Editor

🟥
Place platforms by tapping the canvas. Choose color, width. Use Block Pattern mode to stamp repeating rows.
💾
Press Save to store your level. A toast confirmation appears. Press ▶ Play to test immediately.
🚪
Press Exit in the editor toolbar to return to the main menu at any time.
🔁
Enable Repeatable on saved levels in Custom Levels to have them appear randomly during endless play.
